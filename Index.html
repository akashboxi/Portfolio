Love Universe
3 Pre-Weddings + 1 Wedding · Sense Says Studios
The Concept

Wedding showreels are almost universally forgettable — a montage of kisses, vows, and slow-motion confetti. This one was built to feel like the opening of a cinematic universe.

How I Built It

Structured like a film trailer: story highlights from four separate shoots, emotional continuity across different couples, then a high-paced beat-synced montage at the song's crescendo. Motion graphics layered at the end. Functions as both a proof-of-craft document and a standalone film.

The Approach

Most wedding teasers show the best shots. This one takes one scene — 10–20% of the full film — and tells it with the depth of a short film. A teaser that earns its watch.

Trailers & Narrative Films
Series Trailer · Pocket FM
My Vampire System
Supernatural Microdrama · Pocket FM
The Challenge

Build suspense and desire for a supernatural audio drama series — without giving the story away. Make an audio-first product feel like a visual event worth watching.

The Edit Logic

Structured like an MCU trailer: enough to hook, nothing that spoils. 90s YouTube aesthetic used intentionally for nostalgia contrast. High-paced action cuts with deliberate silence breaks to let tension breathe. The viewer wants to know what happens next — that's the only job.

"A trailer that creates want without creating spoilers. The hardest balance in marketing."
